Introducing Yong-in's famous spots

1.Everland

Everland is South Korea's largest theme park located in Yong-in.

The park is divided into several themed zones, including:

-European Adventure - Designes like a European village, this area features the famous wooden roller coasater, T Express, one of the fastest and steepest in the world.

-Zootopia - A large zoo section where you can see animals from around the world, and ride the Safari World bus to get close to lions, tigers, and bears.

-American Adventure - Inspired by American history and culture, featuring exciting rides.

-Magic Land - A fantasy-themed area perfect for families and children.
