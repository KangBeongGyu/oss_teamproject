Korean Folk Village

The Korean Folk Village is a popular cultural attraction located in Yongin. It offers visitors a unique opportunity to experiecnece traditional Korean life and culture from the Joseon Dynasty period(1392-1897).

The village features meticulously reconstructed traditional houses, workshops, and government offices. Actors dressed in historical costumes reenact daily life, folk games, and traditional ceremonies such as weddings, shaman rituals, and farming practices.

In addition to the cultural displays, the Korean Folk Village offers folk performances including tightrope walking, horseback martial arts, and traditional musice and dance. There are also various hands-on activities like traditional crafts, pottery, and hanbok(traditional Korean clothing) experiences.

With its immersive atmosphere and educational programs, the Korean Folk Village is an ideal destination for both tourists and students who want to learn more about Korea's rich cultural heritage.
